Lemon Rice Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1-2 green chilies (chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger (finely chopped)
  • 8-10 curry leaves
  • 2 tbsp peanuts or cashews
  • 1/2 tsp salt (adjust to taste)
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 tbsp chopped coriander leaves

Instructions

  1. Use cooled cooked rice to prevent it from getting mushy.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add cumin seeds, green chilies, ginger, curry leaves, and peanuts/cashews. Saut√© until golden.
  4. Add turmeric powder and salt. Stir well.
  5. Add the cooked rice and mix gently.
  6. Turn off the heat and add fresh lemon juice.
  7. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve warm.
